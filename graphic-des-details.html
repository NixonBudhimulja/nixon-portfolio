Graphic Design

Category: Graphic Design

Various Projects

These projects were created for school and university assignments. The content consists of posters I designed, which I believe are good enough to showcase. I created these posters solo using Figma and Photoshop. The main challenges I faced were deciding on the layout of text and images and selecting the right colors. However, I overcame these issues by seeking inspiration online and using color palettes to ensure cohesive designs.

I also made digital artwork that is "skull and flower" themed, this was drew using Procreate. The project is an artwork based on the philosophy of Sapardi Djoko Damono's poetry. His poems depict themes of death and immortality. In my artwork, there is a skull wrapped in a shroud symbolizing death, a flying bird representing a messenger, and a rose signifying both death and life. The brown background symbolizes balance and peace. My role in this project was as a solo digital artist, although I received some assistance from more senior digital artists. The challenge was figuring out how to start, but with help from seniors, I was able to complete this solo project successfully.

Organization / Event Projects

This is one of my best project due to the significant design responsibilities I handled for a major BNCC (Bina Nusantara Computer Club) event. The CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility) event was a webinar on "The Secret of Building Startups in the Digital Age." My role included designing the CSR logo, virtual background, event microblog, quiz content, form header, and certificate. The challenge was managing many designs, which was overwhelming. However, by collaborating with my DnD (Design and Documentation) team, we successfully completed all the designs.
