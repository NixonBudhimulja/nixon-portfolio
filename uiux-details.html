Pendekinlink.id

From a UI/UX perspective, the design of pendekinlink.id focused heavily on simplicity and ease of use. The goal was to create a clean, minimalistic interface that allows users to shorten links without unnecessary complexity. In the initial stages, we conducted user research to identify pain points in existing URL shorteners and designed wireframes in Figma based on those insights. Each feature, such as link customization and login/signup functionality, was designed with accessibility in mind, ensuring that users could navigate the platform effortlessly. We incorporated user feedback throughout the design iterations to refine both aesthetics and functionality, making the overall experience smoother and more intuitive.


Sea of Ftars

For the UI/UX of the Sea of Ftars project, my focus was on creating a visually immersive experience that resonates with the game's fantasy theme. The design process began with wireframing in Figma, where I explored layouts that could best present game-related content, such as character details, fan art, and story elements. One of the key challenges was balancing aesthetics with usability, ensuring that the design was engaging yet easy to navigate for both gamers and casual visitors. I incorporated elements like rich visuals, clear navigation menus, and interactive components to enhance user engagement. Additionally, attention was paid to creating responsive layouts that function seamlessly across desktop, providing a consistent user experience. The color palette and typography choices were inspired by the game's original design, aligning the website's look and feel with the game itself. User testing helped refine the interface, ensuring that it not only looked great but was also intuitive to use, creating a smooth journey from page to page.

Es Krim Tiga Rasa

The "Es Krim Tiga Rasa" website was designed by me with a vibrant, colorful theme to match the playful nature of ice cream. The overall UI/UX emphasizes a cheerful and inviting experience, using bright, bold colors that appeal to users immediately upon visiting. The homepage features a prominent "Order Now" button, guiding users smoothly through the process while maintaining a clear and straightforward navigation. The design also highlights the different ice cream flavors with appealing visuals, making it easy for users to explore and select their favorites. Additionally, the website includes sections like "About Us" and "Contact Us," incorporating ice cream-themed images that reinforce the brand identity and enhance the user experience. The combination of playful design elements and intuitive navigation creates a user-friendly, enjoyable interaction with the site.
